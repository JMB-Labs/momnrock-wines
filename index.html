Our Story

Our Story

A legacy carried from a Spanish vineyard
to a Filipino table.

Momnrock Luxury Wines is a family-owned private-label wine house founded in 2018 by Jenny Bonnevie-Bautista. The year before, in 2017, Jenny travelled to Spain and walked the vineyards her late father had walked before her — retracing a legacy carried for decades at the family table.

With Spanish winemakers at her side, she crafted a custom blend with the Filipino palate in mind: tuning the ratio of Garnacha to Tempranillo to reward the softer, naturally sweet tongue of Filipino drinkers. Generous fruit and gentle body come first; quiet structure follows.

Every bottle since has carried that small inheritance forward — a private label, made for family, friends, and the kind of evenings that deserve a good story in a glass.

Jenny B. Bautista
Founder  ·  Momnrock Luxury Wines

Selección Privada
Tempranillo Garnacha

Produced & bottled in Spain · 12% Alc

Ruby red, soft at the rim. Ripe cherry and dark plum on the nose, a touch of something earthier underneath. Garnacha does the talking on the palate — round fruit, gentle sweetness, easy to drink — while Tempranillo holds the line behind it.

Pairs With Grilled lamb · Iberico ham · Kaldereta · Aged Manchego
Australia  ·  Curated

Betheone
Shiraz

Australian Shiraz · Bold, food-friendly

Deep crimson with violet edges. Generous on the nose — ripe blackberry, dark plum, blueberry, cracked black pepper, and a whisper of vanilla oak. Full-bodied and silky, with plush tannins carrying the wine to a warm, peppery finish.

Pairs With Grilled rib-eye · Lamb chops · Crispy pata · Lechon
Spain  ·  Curated

Prospero
Moscato

Sparkling Moscato Espumoso · Produced in Spain

Radiant golden yellow, crowned by a steady, fine stream of bubbles. A floral garden on the nose — jasmine, white blossoms, ripe apricot — layered with sun-warmed peach and a gentle touch of honey. Light, airy, and charmingly fruity.

Pairs With Brunch · Leche flan · Mango float · Fresh oysters
